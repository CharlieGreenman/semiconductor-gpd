## Narrative Arc

v6.0 ranked Hg-family cuprates as primary and nickelates as secondary for closing the 149 K gap. v7.0 converted those rankings into experiment-ready protocols for both routes. Phase 24 assembled a layered Hg1223 PQP reproduction protocol from three sources (primary paper, FeSe/BST methodology, Phase 19 runbook) with a 131 K success gate and pre-registered 12-class outcome-to-decision map. Phase 25 consolidated an 11-point strain-Tc data table for bilayer La3Ni2O7-class films, wrote a mapping protocol with an 80 K success gate, and produced a 5-gate promotion framework — but found that current data (best zero-resist 40 K) is far below all action thresholds. Phase 26 integrated both tracks into a unified route-confidence assessment, confirming that the route ranking is unchanged, the 149 K gap is unchanged, and the next milestone must execute protocols rather than design more.
