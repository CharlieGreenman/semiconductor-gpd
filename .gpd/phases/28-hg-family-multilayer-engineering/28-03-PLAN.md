<objective>
Compute phonon dispersion, electron-phonon coupling, and phonon-only Eliashberg Tc for Hg1234, and compare with the Hg1223 baseline from Phase 27.

Purpose: The phonon-only Tc provides a lower bound (~20% of total cuprate Tc). While absolute values are unreliable for cuprates, the RELATIVE TREND between Hg1223 and Hg1234 phonon-only Tc tracks the phonon contribution to the layer-count dependence. If phonon-only Tc increases from Hg1223 to Hg1234, the phonon channel favors more layers.

Output: Phonon dispersion, alpha2F, lambda, omega_log, Allen-Dynes and Eliashberg Tc for Hg1234, with quantitative comparison to Hg1223 values.
</objective>
