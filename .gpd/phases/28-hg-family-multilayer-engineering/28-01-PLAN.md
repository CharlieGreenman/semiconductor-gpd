<objective>
Construct the Hg1234 (HgBa2Ca3Cu4O10+delta) crystal structure and prepare complete DFT electronic structure inputs, with literature-grounded expected outputs.

Purpose: Hg1234 is the 4-CuO2-layer member of the Hg-family series. Adding one more CuO2 layer beyond Hg1223 introduces 2 inner planes + 2 outer planes (vs 1 inner + 2 outer in Hg1223). The inner planes have higher local Tc. Does this structural change increase or decrease the bulk Tc? This plan establishes the structural and electronic baseline.

Output: Complete QE input files (vc-relax, SCF, bands, NSCF, DOS), analysis scripts, literature-expected structure and electronic properties, comparison with Hg1223 baseline.
