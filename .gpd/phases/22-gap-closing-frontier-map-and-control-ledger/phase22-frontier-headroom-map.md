# Phase 22 Frontier Headroom Map

This map compares the surviving post-`v5.0` route families on one common basis: best current `Tc`, operating state, retention status, evidence depth, and route value for shrinking the remaining room-temperature gap.

## Route Comparison

| Route family | Best current benchmark | Operating state of that benchmark | Evidence depth | Headroom verdict | Route verdict |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `Hg`-family cuprates | `151 K` retained at ambient pressure after pressure quench in `Hg1223`; broader family headroom reaches `153 K` zero-resist and `166 K` onset under pressure | retained ambient for the carried `Hg1223` benchmark; higher family ceiling still pressure-assisted | strong for `Hg1223`, moderate for broader family transfer | highest absolute non-hydride headroom in the carried set | stays the primary gap-closing route family |
| nickelates | ambient film onset near `63 K`, ambient bulk benchmark near `40 K`, and current high-pressure single-crystal frontier up to `96 K` | mixed: ambient film and bulk routes exist, but best current frontier is still pressure-assisted | rapidly improving, but more fragmented than `Hg1223` | lower absolute headroom than `Hg` cuprates, but rising faster | strongest secondary route family and active expansion target |
| conventional near-ambient controls | current evidence and ceiling work do not outrank the unconventional frontier; near-ambient conventional routes remain well below room temperature and generally below the carried `Hg1223` scale | mostly theory-led or far below room temperature in practical evidence | weaker than the unconventional frontier for this goal | insufficient to beat the unconventional leaders on headroom | useful control set, not a post-`v5.0` primary route |

## Headroom Interpretation

- `Hg`-family cuprates still matter because they keep the smallest known gap to room temperature in the carried set.
- Nickelates now matter because they combine nontrivial `Tc` growth with multiple active knobs: pressure, strain, oxygen control, and chemical pressure.
- Conventional near-ambient routes still matter as controls, but they do not currently offer a better route-expansion target than the unconventional frontier.

## Guardrail

The best carried retained benchmark is still `Hg1223` at `151 K`, which remains `149 K` below `300 K`. Nothing in this map changes that fact.
