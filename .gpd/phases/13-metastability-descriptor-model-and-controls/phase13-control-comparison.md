## Separation Verdict

The scorecard does separate the routes in the way the milestone needs:

- retained-ambient positives cluster at `medium-high` or `high` retention-friendliness
- hydride negatives cluster at `low`

The separation is not driven by `Tc` alone. `CsInH3` has a better raw `Tc` than several ambient positives, but it fails on ambient access, structural memory, and evidence depth.
