# Plan 46-01: Stability Assessment and Missing Physics

## Objective

CR-03 triggered: 3 candidates predict Tc > 200 K. Assess stability (E_hull, phonon stability)
AND provide honest missing-physics inventory for the uncertainty budget.

## Tasks

### Task 1: Stability assessment for top 200 K+ candidates
type="auto" verify="analytical"

2. Hg1223 at 30 GPa (231 K): same

### Task 2: Missing physics and uncertainty budget
type="auto" verify="analytical"

Even with CR-03 triggered, the predictions carry large uncertainties.
Document: vertex corrections, dynamic U, cluster size, analytic continuation errors.
Quantify which missing physics could reduce or increase the predicted Tc.
