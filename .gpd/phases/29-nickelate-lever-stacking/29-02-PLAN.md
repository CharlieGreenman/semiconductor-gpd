<objective>
Compute the La3Ni2O7 electronic structure under -1% and -2% compressive epitaxial strain (corresponding to LAO and SLAO substrates) and compare with the unstrained baseline to identify strain-dependent trends in Ni-dz2 orbital weight and N(E_F).

Purpose: NI-01 completion (3 strain points). Strain modifies c/a and thus the sigma-bonding dz2-O_pz overlap that controls bilayer nickelate superconductivity. Identifying the electronic structure trend with strain is essential before computing electron-phonon coupling (Plan 29-03).
Output: Constrained relaxation inputs for 2 strain points, strain-dependent electronic structure comparison, dz2 weight trend as function of strain.
</objective>
