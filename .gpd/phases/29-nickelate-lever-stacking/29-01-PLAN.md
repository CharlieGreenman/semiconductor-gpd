    1. Crystal structure: La3Ni2O7 in the Amam orthorhombic phase (ambient pressure, T < 115 K).

       - For DFT with PBEsol, the tetragonal I4/mmm parent is also acceptable (a ~ 3.833 A, c ~ 20.5 A); document which is used.
       - Unit cell contains 2 formula units (Z=2): 6 La, 4 Ni, 14 O = 24 atoms per cell.
       - Bilayer structure: two NiO2 planes separated by a bridging (inner apical) O, with La/O rock-salt blocking layers.
       - Use the I4/mmm parent structure for computational efficiency (fewer atoms, higher symmetry), noting that the Amam tilt pattern may lower energy by ~5-20 meV/f.u.
    2. Pseudopotentials: ONCV scalar-relativistic for La, Ni, O from SG15 or PseudoDojo.
       - La: 5s2 5p6 5d1 6s2 (11 valence electrons)
       - Ni: 3s2 3p6 3d8 4s2 (18 valence electrons) -- include semi-core 3s3p
       - O: 2s2 2p4 (6 valence electrons)
    3. QE vc-relax input:
       - ibrav = 0 (explicit CELL_PARAMETERS)
       - ecutwfc = 80 Ry, ecutrho = 640 Ry (ONCV recommendation for Ni semi-core)
       - k-point grid: 8x8x2 (tetragonal) or 6x6x2 (orthorhombic) with Monkhorst-Pack, shifted
       - Marzari-Vanderbilt cold smearing, degauss = 0.02 Ry (for metallic system)
       - cell_dofree = 'all' for full relaxation
       - forc_conv_thr = 1.0d-4, press_conv_thr = 0.5
       - nspin = 1 (non-spin-polarized; paramagnetic metal above T_N ~ 150 K)
    4. Literature-expected relaxed structure (PBEsol):
       - a_calc ~ 3.82-3.85 A (within 0.5% of 3.833 A experimental)
       - c_calc ~ 20.3-20.7 A (within 1% of 20.5 A experimental)
       - c/a ~ 5.3-5.4
       - Inner apical O-Ni-O bond angle ~ 168-174 deg (tilt in Amam)
       - Ni-O apical distance (inner) ~ 1.96-2.02 A
       - Ni-O planar distance ~ 1.92-1.94 A
    5. Write structure_comparison.py: compare relaxed structure with experiment, compute % errors, flag if > 2%.
    6. Write la327_unstrained_structure.json: lattice parameters, internal coordinates, space group, comparison metrics.

    CRITICAL: The bilayer nickelate has TWO distinct apical oxygen positions:
    - Inner apical O (bridging the bilayer): controls sigma-bonding strength
    - Outer apical O (between bilayer and La-O block): controls charge transfer
    Do NOT confuse these. Label them explicitly in atomic positions.

       a. Band structure topology (from Sun et al. PRL 2023, Luo et al. PRL 2023, Lechermann PRX 2023):
          - Two electron-like Fermi surface sheets (gamma and beta): Ni-dz2 sigma-bonding and antibonding
          - gamma sheet: bonding combination of dz2 across the bilayer, around Gamma
          - beta sheet: antibonding combination of dz2, around M/A
          - alpha sheet: Ni-dx2-y2 character, large 2D cylinder
          - Total: 3 main Fermi surface sheets

          - N(E_F) ~ 3.5-5.0 states/eV/cell (both spins) from published PBEsol/PBE
          - Ni-d contribution: ~60-70% of N(E_F)
          - Ni-dz2: ~25-35% of N(E_F) -- this is the KEY orbital for bilayer SC
          - Ni-dx2-y2: ~25-35% of N(E_F)
          - O-p: ~20-30% of N(E_F)
       c. Fermi surface nesting: near (pi, pi) nesting vector connects alpha sheets (SDW instability)

    6. Write la327_unstrained_electronic.json: N(E_F), orbital weights, band count at E_F, Fermi surface sheet descriptions

    KEY PHYSICS: The sigma-bonding band (dz2 bonding across the bilayer via inner apical O) is what distinguishes La3Ni2O7 from infinite-layer nickelates. Its weight at E_F controls the interlayer pairing channel. Strain modifies c/a and thus the dz2-O_pz overlap, directly affecting this weight.
