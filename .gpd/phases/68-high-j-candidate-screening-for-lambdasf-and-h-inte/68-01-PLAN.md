# Phase 68: High-J Candidate Screening for lambda_sf and H-Intercalation

## Objective

Since Phase 67 found NO candidate with omega_sf > 500 K in a metallic state, Phase 68 performs the formal closure of Track A. For completeness, we screen the best available candidates (cuprates at omega_sf ~ 350 K, iron pnictides at ~220 K) for lambda_sf, H-intercalation feasibility, and omega_log_eff to quantify exactly how far Track A falls short of the 300 K target.

## Context

- Phase 67 result: No material with dressed omega_sf > 500 K; cuprates are the ceiling at ~350 K
- v12.0 baseline: omega_log_eff = 483 K, Tc = 197 K at mu*=0, lambda_total = 3.5
- Target: omega_log_eff = 740 K for Tc = 300 K
- Combined formula: omega_log_eff = exp[(lambda_ph * ln(omega_ph) + lambda_sf * ln(omega_sf)) / lambda_total]
