# Phase 83: Plasmon Spectrum Survey in Layered Metals

## Objective

Survey layered metals for low-energy plasmons (omega_pl < 1 eV) that could
mediate electronic pairing. Characterize dielectric response and select
candidates for Phase 84 pairing calculation.

## Context

- Plasmon energy omega_pl = sqrt(4*pi*n*e^2/m) typically 0.5-20 eV for metals
- For pairing: need omega_pl < 1 eV (comparable to phonon energy scale)
- Cuprate c-axis plasmon at 10-50 meV already exists in high-Tc materials
- Key question: can plasmon-mediated pairing ADD to phonon pairing?

## Tasks

### Task 1: Compute plasmon energies for candidate families (type="auto")

For each candidate family, compute or collect from literature:
- Carrier density n, effective mass m*, dimensionality
- Bare plasmon energy omega_pl(q=0)
- Screening effects: Thomas-Fermi screening, interlayer coupling
Tabulate: material | n (cm^-3) | m*/m_e | omega_pl (meV) | Tc_expt (K) | dimensionality

### Task 2: Select candidates for Phase 84 (type="auto")

Select at least 2 candidates with omega_pl < 1 eV AND finite N(E_F).
Justify selection based on (1) low omega_pl, (2) existing superconductivity,
(3) feasibility of plasmon pairing calculation.

## Success Criteria

- At least 6 material families surveyed
- omega_pl computed for each
- At least 2 candidates with omega_pl < 1 eV selected
- Dielectric properties tabulated
