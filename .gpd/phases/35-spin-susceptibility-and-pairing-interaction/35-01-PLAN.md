# Phase 35, Plan 01: Spin Susceptibility and Pairing Interaction for Hg1223

## Objective

Extract the spin susceptibility chi(q,omega) from the Phase 34 DMFT solution, compute the spin-fluctuation-mediated pairing interaction V_sf, determine the coupling constant lambda_sf in the d-wave channel, and verify the pairing sign (VALD-03). Use the DMFT self-energy (Z=0.33) combined with literature-grounded DMFT chi values for optimally doped cuprates. NO HPC required -- design workflow + literature-grounded numerical computation.

## Context

- Phase 34 produced converged DMFT: Z=0.33, m*/m=3.0, U=3.5 eV, J=0.65 eV, W=2.18 eV
- v8.0 phonon lambda_ph = 1.19, omega_log = 25.1 meV (phonon-only Tc = 31 K)
- Spin fluctuations contribute 55-80% of cuprate Tc (v8.0 mechanism analysis)
- Expected: chi peaks at (pi,pi), d-wave pairing, lambda_sf ~ 1-3

## Tasks

### Task 1: Compute bare Lindhard susceptibility chi_0(q,omega)
type="auto"
verify="numerical"

Compute the bare (non-interacting but mass-renormalized) susceptibility chi_0(q,omega) on a 2D Brillouin zone grid from the 3-band tight-binding model with DMFT mass renormalization (Z=0.33). Use the Lindhard formula on a dense k-mesh. Check Kramers-Kronig consistency and that chi_0 is positive.

**Done when:** chi_0(q,omega=0) computed on at least 32x32 q-grid, peak location identified, Kramers-Kronig satisfied.

### Task 2: Compute RPA-enhanced spin susceptibility chi_RPA(q,omega)
type="auto"
verify="numerical"

Apply the RPA enhancement: chi_RPA(q,omega) = chi_0(q,omega) / (1 - U_eff * chi_0(q,omega)) with U_eff calibrated from the DMFT U. Verify the Stoner criterion U_eff*chi_0(Q) < 1 (no magnetic ordering). Check that chi_RPA peaks near (pi,pi) and is enhanced by factor 3-10 over chi_0.

**Done when:** chi_RPA computed, Stoner criterion verified, enhancement factor quantified, peak at (pi,pi) confirmed.

### Task 3: Extract spin-fluctuation pairing interaction V_sf(q,omega)
type="auto"
verify="numerical"

Compute the spin-fluctuation-mediated pairing interaction: V_sf(q) = (3/2) U^2 chi_RPA(q,omega) for the static limit. Decompose V_sf into angular momentum channels (s-wave, d-wave, p-wave) via Fermi surface averaging. Verify d-wave is the leading attractive channel (VALD-03 sign check).

**Done when:** V_sf computed, channel decomposition complete, d-wave identified as leading channel with correct sign.

### Task 4: Compute lambda_sf and combined lambda_total
type="auto"
verify="numerical"

Extract lambda_sf from the d-wave projected V_sf using the standard formula. Combine with v8.0 phonon lambda_ph to get lambda_total. Estimate Tc from McMillan/Allen-Dynes with combined coupling. Compare with 151 K benchmark.

**Done when:** lambda_sf extracted (target 1.0-3.0), lambda_total computed, preliminary Tc estimate provided, all results saved to JSON.

### Task 5: Validation and cross-checks
type="auto"
verify="analytical"

Cross-check: (1) chi satisfies Kramers-Kronig, (2) spectral weight sum rule, (3) lambda_sf consistent with literature values for optimally doped cuprates, (4) d-wave gap symmetry nodes at correct locations, (5) dimensions of all quantities correct.

**Done when:** All 5 cross-checks pass or deviations documented.

## Success Criteria

1. chi(q,omega) peaks near Q = (pi,pi) at low omega
2. V_sf is attractive in d-wave channel (VALD-03)
3. lambda_sf in range 1.0-3.0
4. lambda_total = lambda_ph + lambda_sf > 1.5
5. All quantities have correct dimensions
