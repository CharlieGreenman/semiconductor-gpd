# Phase 18 Research: v4 Route Update And Next-Step Memo

## What Must Be Decided

After Phases `15-17`, the repo has three new things it did not have at the end of `v3.0`:

1. an exact `Hg1223` retained-state window
2. a cross-system `PQP` control map
3. a staged reproducibility campaign with explicit gates

What it still does **not** have is new experimental replication data. So the route update must separate:

- stronger scientific program definition
- stronger control visibility
- unchanged room-temperature gap
- still-unproven reproducibility basin

## Baseline For Comparison

### End of Phase 14 / end of `v3.0`

- `Hg1223` primary route
- bilayer nickelates backup route
- `Hg1223` benchmark-strong but protocol-opaque

### End of Phase 17 / inside `v4.0`

- `Hg1223` remains primary route
- bilayer nickelates remain the backup
- `Hg1223` is now protocol-specified and campaign-defined
- exact `vQ`, broader sample statistics, and experimentally demonstrated basin width remain missing

## Likely Honest Verdict

`Hg1223` should come out **stronger as a research program**, not stronger as a proven platform.

That means:

- upgrade on protocol specificity
- upgrade on control visibility
- upgrade on follow-up experimental clarity
- no upgrade on room-temperature practicality
- only a limited upgrade on route confidence, because the campaign has not yet been executed experimentally

## What The Final Memo Must Preserve

- the `149 K` gap between the best retained `Hg1223` benchmark and room temperature
- the fact that consumer hardware remains unsupported
- the nickelate backup route, because `Hg1223` still lacks proven basin width
- the next experiment set, which should be the new center of effort
