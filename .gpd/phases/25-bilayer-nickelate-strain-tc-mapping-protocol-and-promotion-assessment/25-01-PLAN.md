    - Primary: La3Ni2O7 (baseline for all substrates)
    - Secondary: (La,Pr)3Ni2O7 (demonstrated 63 K onset on SLAO with GAE)
    - Tertiary: La2.82Sr0.18Ni2O7 (Sr-doped variant)
    - Specify that composition and substrate should be varied independently where possible to avoid the strain-vs-chemical-pressure confound (Pitfall 3 from RESEARCH.md)

    SECTION 3 -- GROWTH METHOD:
    - State that growth method (PLD vs GAE) is a major confound: up to 23 K difference on same substrate
    - Require growth method as a controlled and logged variable

    - Require HRXRD RSM confirmation of coherent epitaxy before reporting strain-Tc data

    - Minimum required: four-probe R(T) from 300 K to 2 K + SQUID M(T) and M(H) at T < Tc + HRXRD with RSM

    - R(T) Tc extraction: define onset (first deviation from normal-state extrapolation), midpoint (50% of normal-state R), and zero-resistance (R < noise floor, specify instrument sensitivity)
    - Require reporting all three (onset, midpoint, zero-resist) but use zero-resist for all gate decisions
    - SQUID: Meissner fraction must exceed 10% of full diamagnetic signal to count as bulk SC. Below 10% flag as potential filamentary SC.

    SECTION 5 -- SUCCESS GATE (NIC-02):
    - Primary gate: ambient zero-resistance Tc > 80 K in any bilayer La3Ni2O7-class film
    - The gate uses zero-resistance Tc, NOT onset (forbidden proxy)
    - Operating conditions: ambient pressure (0 GPa), no DAC, no clamped cell
    - Epitaxial strain maintained by substrate is permitted (this is not operating pressure)
    - Confirmation requirement: at least 2 independent groups or 5 samples from one group with consistent results

    SECTION 6 -- ROOM-TEMPERATURE GAP (VALD-02):
    - Project best benchmark: Hg1223 at 151 K zero-resist retained ambient. Gap = 300 - 151 = 149 K.
