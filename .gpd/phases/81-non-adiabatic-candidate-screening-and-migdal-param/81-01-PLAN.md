# Phase 81: Non-Adiabatic Candidate Screening and Migdal Parameter Survey

## Objective

Screen known superconductors for Migdal breakdown (omega_D/E_F > 0.3). Tabulate
candidates, identify the best target for Phase 82 vertex correction calculation.

## Context

- v14.0 established 240 +/- 30 K Eliashberg ceiling
- v11.0 open question: is Migdal valid when omega_log > 800 K?
- Non-adiabatic vertex corrections can ENHANCE or SUPPRESS Tc

## Tasks

### Task 1: Compile Migdal parameter table (type="auto")

Collect omega_D (or omega_LO for optical-phonon systems), E_F, and omega_D/E_F for:
- MATBG (magic-angle twisted bilayer graphene)
- SrTiO3 (dilute carrier limit)
- FeSe/STO interface
- Hydrogen-rich clathrates at moderate pressure
- Flat-band hydrides (predicted)

Tabulate: material | omega_D (meV) | E_F (meV) | omega_D/E_F | Tc_expt (K) | Tc_Eliashberg (K) | discrepancy

### Task 2: Rank and select best candidate (type="auto")

Rank by: (1) magnitude of Migdal breakdown, (2) existing Tc discrepancy, (3) relevance to 300 K goal.
Select best candidate for Phase 82 with justification.

## Success Criteria

- At least 5 materials with omega_D/E_F computed
- At least 3 with omega_D/E_F > 0.3
- Best candidate identified with justification
- Eliashberg Tc discrepancy noted for all candidates with data
