# Plan 38-01: Nickelate RPA Spin Susceptibility

## Objective

Compute the bare and RPA-enhanced spin susceptibility chi_RPA(q,omega) for La3Ni2O7
at 0% and -2% compressive strain, extract the spin-fluctuation pairing interaction V_sf
and coupling constant lambda_sf in the leading pairing channel (s+/- expected for bilayer).

## Context

- v8.0 established phonon-only Tc ceiling of 22-26 K for La3Ni2O7
- Phonon lambda_ph = 0.58 (0%) and 0.92 (-2%) from @data/nickelate/tc_strain_results.json
- Electronic structure from @data/nickelate/la327_unstrained_electronic.json and @data/nickelate/la327_strain_comparison.json
- Three Fermi surface sheets: gamma (dz2 bonding), beta (dz2 antibonding), alpha (dx2-y2)
- Bilayer sigma-bonding splitting: 0.80 eV (0%), 1.05 eV (-2%)
- Published RPA results for La3Ni2O7: lambda_sf ~ 0.5-1.5 depending on U and method

## Tasks

### Task 1: Bare Lindhard Susceptibility chi_0(q,omega=0)
type="auto" verify="numerical"

Construct a tight-binding model for the 3-band (gamma, beta, alpha) Fermi surface
of La3Ni2O7 and compute the bare Lindhard susceptibility:

chi_0(q) = -sum_k,n,m [ f(E_n(k)) - f(E_m(k+q)) ] / [ E_n(k) - E_m(k+q) + i*delta ]

on a 48x48 q-mesh in the 2D Brillouin zone at omega=0 and T=50 K.

**Done criteria:**
- chi_0(q) computed on 48x48 mesh for both strain states
- Peak structure identified (expect nesting-driven peak near (pi,pi) or (pi,0))
- N(E_F) from chi_0(q=0) cross-checked against DFT value
- Dimensions verified: chi_0 in states/eV

### Task 2: RPA Enhancement, V_sf, and lambda_sf
type="auto" verify="numerical"

Apply RPA enhancement:
  chi_RPA(q) = chi_0(q) / (1 - U * chi_0(q))

with U scanned over [1.5, 2.0, 2.5, 3.0] eV (literature range for nickelates).

Extract pairing interaction in s+/- and d-wave channels:
  V_sf(q) = (3/2) U^2 chi_RPA(q) - (1/2) U
  lambda_sf = N(E_F) * <V_sf * g_k * g_{k+q}> (projected onto pairing channel)

where g_k is the gap symmetry form factor.

**Done criteria:**
- Stoner criterion checked: U*max(chi_0) < 1 (or document magnetic instability)
- chi_RPA peak enhancement quantified
- V_sf extracted in s+/- and d-wave channels
- lambda_sf computed for each U value and both strains
- Sign check: lambda_sf > 0 in the leading attractive channel (VALD-03)
- Strain comparison: lambda_sf(-2%) vs lambda_sf(0%) quantified

## Success Criteria

1. chi_0(q) peaks near expected nesting vector
2. RPA enhancement without Stoner divergence for at least U <= 2.5 eV
3. Leading pairing channel identified with attractive V_sf
4. lambda_sf > 0.3 for at least one strain/U combination
5. Strain effect on lambda_sf quantified
