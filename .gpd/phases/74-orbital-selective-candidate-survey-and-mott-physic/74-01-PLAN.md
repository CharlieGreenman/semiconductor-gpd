# Phase 74 Plan: Orbital-Selective Candidate Survey and Mott Physics Assessment

## Objective

Survey multi-orbital correlated materials for orbital-selective Mott physics -- where one orbital is highly correlated (d-wave pairing channel, Z < 0.3) and another is itinerant (phonon coupling channel, Z > 0.5). Tabulate candidates with orbital-resolved quasiparticle weights, magnetic exchange, d-wave plausibility, and phonon coupling feasibility.

## Context

- v13.0 established: 300 K requires lambda_ph >= 3.0 + d-wave (mu*=0) + omega_log_eff >= 740 K
- The core tension: d-wave requires correlations (large U/W), but correlations produce SF that drag omega_log_eff down
- Orbital selectivity could resolve this: correlated orbital provides d-wave, itinerant orbital couples to phonons
- Cuprates have ONE orbital doing both -- that is the problem

## Tasks

### Task 1: Iron Pnictide/Chalcogenide Survey (type="auto")
Survey Fe-based superconductors (FeSe, LaFeAsO, BaFe2As2) for orbital selectivity in the t2g manifold.
Extract: Z per orbital (dxy, dxz, dyz), J values, pairing symmetry, Tc.
**done:** Table of Fe-based candidates with orbital-resolved Z, J, and pairing assessment.

### Task 2: Ruthenate Survey (type="auto")
Survey Sr2RuO4 and Ca2-xSrxRuO4 for orbital selectivity in t2g orbitals.
Extract: Z per orbital, van Hove proximity, pairing symmetry.
**done:** Table of ruthenate candidates with orbital assessment.

### Task 3: Nickelate Survey (type="auto")
Survey infinite-layer nickelates (NdNiO2, LaNiO2) and Ruddlesden-Popper nickelates (La4Ni3O10, La3Ni2O7).
Extract: Z for dx2-y2 vs dz2, J, d-wave plausibility, phonon coupling in itinerant orbital.
**done:** Table of nickelate candidates with two-orbital assessment.

### Task 4: Additional Families (type="auto")
Survey cobaltates (NaxCoO2), osmium oxides (Cd2Os2O7), and heavy fermion systems (CeCoIn5).
Extract: orbital selectivity evidence, Z per orbital, J, pairing.
**done:** Table of additional candidates.

### Task 5: Consolidated Ranking and Assessment (type="auto")
Produce master candidate table with columns: compound | structure | correlated orbital (Z) | itinerant orbital (Z) | J_corr (meV) | d-wave plausible? | phonon coupling expected? | overall OS score.
Identify top 2-3 candidates for Phase 75 detailed computation.
**done:** Master ranking table and top candidate list with physics justification.

## Verification

- Each family must have at least one compound with literature or computed Z values
- d-wave assessment must reference lattice geometry and exchange pathway
- Phonon coupling assessment must reference DOS at E_F for itinerant orbital
- At least 3 candidates in the final table
