# Phase 58: Inverse Eliashberg Target Map for 300 K

## Objective

Compute the (lambda_total, omega_log) contour that yields Tc = 300 K using modified Allen-Dynes with strong-coupling corrections and d-wave mu*=0. Place known materials on this map and identify the target zone for hydrogen-correlated oxides.

## Context

- v11.0 validated Eliashberg solver: 146 K vs 151 K experimental (3% error)
- v11.0 key numbers: lambda_sf_inf = 2.70, omega_log ~400 K for cuprates
- v11.0 conclusion: omega_log ~400 K is the bottleneck capping Tc at ~200 K
- v1.0 hydride benchmarks: H3S at 182 K (10.5% error), LaH10 at 276 K (10.6% error)
- Convention: explicit hbar and k_B; SI-derived reporting (K, GPa, eV, meV)

## Tasks
