# Phase 90: Flat-Band Materials Survey and Bandwidth Characterization

## Objective
Survey materials with flat bands near E_F, characterize bandwidth W and E_F, estimate Migdal parameter omega_D/E_F, and identify prime candidates for hydrogen-hosting non-adiabatic superconductors.

## Tasks

1. **Survey flat-band material families** (type="auto")
   Survey 5+ families: moire, kagome, Lieb lattice, heavy-fermion hydrides, VHS systems

2. **Identify prime candidates** (type="auto")
   Filter for omega_D/E_F > 1.5 AND H-compatible

3. **Detailed RE-H2 assessment** (type="auto")
   Pressure-dependent W, E_F, omega_D for LaH2, YH2, ScH2, CeH2

4. **Migdal ratio landscape** (type="auto")
   Compare RE-H2 family at 15 GPa

5. **Candidate ranking** (type="auto")
   Score and rank all candidates for Phase 91
