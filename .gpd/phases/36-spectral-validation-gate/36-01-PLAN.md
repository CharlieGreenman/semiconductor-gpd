# Plan 36-01: Spectral Validation Gate for Hg1223 DMFT

## Objective

Validate the Phase 34 DMFT spectral function against known cuprate ARPES features.
This is a MANDATORY GATE (VALD-01): at least 3 of 4 criteria must pass or Phase 37 is BLOCKED.

## Context

@data/hg1223/dmft/dmft_results.json — Converged DMFT results (Z=0.33, m*/m=3.0)
@data/hg1223/spin_susceptibility/pairing_results.json — d-wave channel confirmed
@data/hg1223/dmft/model_params.json — 3-band Hubbard model parameters
@data/hg1223/spin_susceptibility/chi_results.json — chi(q) on 64x64 grid
@.gpd/phases/34-dmft-setup/34-01-SUMMARY.md — DMFT setup summary

## Known ARPES Reference Values (optimally doped cuprates)

- Z_ARPES ~ 0.25-0.35 (ARPES quasiparticle weight)
- Pseudogap ~ 30-50 meV at antinode (pi,0)
- Nodal Fermi velocity ~ 2.0 eV*A (hbar*v_F)
- Lower Hubbard band ~ -1.5 to -2.0 eV below E_F (visible in PES)
- Upper Hubbard band ~ +2.0 to +3.0 eV above E_F
- d-wave gap symmetry: nodes along (0,0)-(pi,pi), max gap at (pi,0)
- Fermi surface: large hole-like barrel centered at (pi,pi), nesting near (pi,0)

## Tasks

### Task 1: Pseudogap at antinodal region [type="auto", verify="analytical"]
**Objective:** Check whether DMFT A(k,omega) shows reduced spectral weight near (pi,0) relative to the nodal direction.

**Method:** Using the converged DMFT self-energy, compute A(k,omega) at the antinodal point k=(pi,0) and at the nodal point k=(pi/2,pi/2). Compare the spectral weight at E_F. The pseudogap signature is a suppression of spectral weight at (pi,0) relative to the nodal region.

**Done criteria:** Quantitative comparison of A(k_AN, E_F) vs A(k_N, E_F); ratio < 1 indicates pseudogap; compare suppression magnitude with ARPES pseudogap of 30-50 meV.

### Task 2: Mott-proximity spectral weight transfer [type="auto", verify="numerical"]
**Objective:** Verify that the DMFT spectral function shows transfer of spectral weight to Hubbard bands, indicating Mott proximity rather than simple metallic behavior.

**Method:** Compute A(omega) integrated over k. Identify the coherent quasiparticle peak near E_F and the lower/upper Hubbard bands. Quantify the fraction of spectral weight in the coherent peak vs Hubbard bands.

**Done criteria:** Lower Hubbard band visible at negative energy (expected ~ -1.5 to -3.5 eV); coherent fraction ~ Z = 0.33; incoherent weight ~ 1-Z = 0.67.

### Task 3: d-wave gap symmetry verification [type="auto", verify="analytical"]
**Objective:** Confirm that the pairing interaction produces gap nodes along the (0,0)-(pi,pi) diagonal and maximum gap at the antinodes.

**Method:** Use the d-wave pairing results from Phase 35 (V_d_wave < 0, leading attractive channel = d-wave). Compute the angular dependence of the gap function Delta(phi) = Delta_0 * cos(2*phi) on the Fermi surface. Verify nodes at phi = pi/4 (nodal direction) and maxima at phi = 0 (antinodal direction).

**Done criteria:** Gap function changes sign at 4 points on Fermi surface; cos(2*phi) symmetry confirmed; maximum gap magnitude estimated and compared with ARPES antinodal gap.

### Task 4: Z and Fermi surface consistency with ARPES [type="auto", verify="numerical"]
**Objective:** Compare DMFT quasiparticle weight Z and Fermi surface topology with ARPES-derived values.

**Method:** (a) Compare Z = 0.33 from DMFT with ARPES Z ~ 0.25-0.35. (b) Compute the Fermi surface from the DMFT-renormalized band structure and compare topology with ARPES (large hole-like barrel at (pi,pi)). (c) Estimate nodal Fermi velocity from renormalized bands and compare with ARPES v_F ~ 2.0 eV*A.

**Done criteria:** Z within 30% of ARPES range center (0.30); Fermi surface is hole-like centered at (pi,pi); v_F within 30% of 2.0 eV*A.

### Task 5: Gate verdict and documentation [type="auto"]
**Objective:** Evaluate the 3-of-4 gate condition and produce the validation report.

**Method:** Tally pass/fail for all 4 criteria. If >= 3 pass, gate is OPEN and Phase 37 proceeds. If < 3 pass, gate is CLOSED and Phase 37 is BLOCKED.

**Done criteria:** Explicit gate verdict (PASS/FAIL) with justification for each criterion. Validation report written. If gate passes, readiness statement for Phase 37.

## Verification / Success Criteria

1. Pseudogap check: spectral weight at (pi,0) is suppressed relative to node
2. Hubbard band check: incoherent spectral weight visible at |omega| > 1 eV
3. d-wave check: gap has cos(2*phi) symmetry with nodes on diagonal
4. Z/FS check: Z within 30% of ARPES and Fermi surface topology matches

**Gate condition:** >= 3 of 4 pass => Phase 37 unlocked. < 3 pass => Phase 37 BLOCKED.
